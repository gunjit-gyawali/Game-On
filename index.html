Game-On


YS: A game made in your favorite game engine (Use Godot if you are beginner)

WS: Get free food!



Who Can Participate?

All Hack Clubbers who are 18 or under, or still in high school, can join this event!

What Is Game-On?

Game-On is a game development event where you can create and submit your own game for a chance to win food grant! Whether you're a beginner or an experienced game developer, this event is open to all skill levels. See the requirements page for more information.

Any Questions?

Join the #game-on channel on Hack Club Slack to connect with fellow participants and mentors!


Club/Event Questions?: Send your question in #game-on on the Hack Club Slack for quick assistance.
